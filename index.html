This project is an evolving Internet Safety Toolkit developed by Dewan Mahmud, a student of Per Scholas. It began as an IP Address Tracker capable of displaying location details, ISP information, and timezone data, but the vision goes far beyond that.

I am planning to expand this platform with features such as Ping Testing, Phone Number Lookup, and Scam-Prevention Utilities to help users detect suspicious activity, protect themselves online, and remain aware of who or what is connecting to them. My goal is to transform this tool into a practical digital security resource that makes everyday internet use safer, smarter, and more secure for everyone.

This project has opened my mind to how powerful API services can be when turned into real-world tools. It inspired me to explore new ways to build solutions that can benefit everyday users, support digital safety awareness, and help people protect themselves against online threats.

All credit, respect, and gratitude goes to my Per Scholas instructors Tishana Trainor and Bryan Santos - two of the best teachers who continuously guide, inspire, and shape my journey in software development.
